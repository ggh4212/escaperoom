Missions

Stealing the Flying Dutchman's Treasure New

You are Blue-Beard, the most revered pirate in the seven seas. The only ship that could ever have any potential of beating you in a ship battle is the Flying Dutchman’s ship. So when they steal your treasure as you dock to sleep in an inn for the night. You must retrieve it so as not to lose your reputation as “The Most Feared Pirate.”

Prison Break

You have been accused of 1st-degree murder and don't plan on doing your required sentence. On your way to your cell, you noticed a few weak points in the prison’s security. You spend the following week planning your escape. Next thing you know, your plan is set and it is time to break out of prison. Can you manage to get out unnoticed and unharmed or will you be sent back to your cell with more security and less of a chance of getting out?

Breaking into Fort Knox

Your mission is to break into Fort Knox and steal the gold that resides inside of it. This is one of the most heavily guarded and secured places in the United States. Can you handle the challenge?
